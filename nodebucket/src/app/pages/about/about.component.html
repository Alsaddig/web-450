nodebucket 101
Facts about our task management application

nodebucket is an employee management system where you can keep track of your tasks. It allows you to log in and view both the tasks that you have yet to complete as well as the ones that have already been completed. Not only does nodebucket give you the ability to view these tasks, it also allows you to create new ones, update them, and delete them.

nodebucket offers a simple and easy-to-use drag-and-drop system where you can quickly reorder your tasks. Once you’ve completed a task, you’re able to drag the task itself and drop it into the “done” column. That way you can efficiently change the status of your tasks without the hassle of having to type anything at all.

nodebucket is great for employees needing to keep track of their tasks, but it’s also good for anyone who benefit from being able to do this as well. You don’t have to be an employee to use it. In fact, it’s extremely useful for those looking to better manage their time and visualize all of the things they need to get done within a certain time frame.

nodebucket is built using something called the MEAN stack, which is basically a very efficient method of programming that utilizes four tried-and-true technologies within the web development world. With the help of MongoDB, Express, Angular, and Node.js the nodebucket application remains safe and simple, giving you peace of mind that all of your user-made content will be secure and easy to access at your convenience.
